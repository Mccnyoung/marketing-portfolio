Pride Campaign

In celebration of Pride Month, Kiss Products launched the Pride Collection and collaborated with The Trevor Project, the LGBTQ+ nonprofit organization which works to prevent suicide among LGBTQ+ youth. Through this collaboration, KISS committed to donating $50,000, emphasizing the brand's social responsibility.

The key visuals of the campaign featured the rainbow, the iconic color of Pride, in a gradient, while highlighting the donation to The Trevor Project to make it clear that consumer purchases would support a meaningful cause.

Additionally, a mouse-over interaction was added to display information about the products worn by the model, encouraging immediate purchases. Through this strategic approach, total sales of $52,718 were achieved.
