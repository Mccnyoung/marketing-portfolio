Armani Beauty's Foundation Online Sales Strategy

Armani Beauty had traditionally sold its flagship product, foundation, through offline channels. However, due to the pandemic, digital transformation became inevitable. Consumers, however, faced difficulties in selecting the right shade online, which led to a significant increase in color-related inquiries.

To address this, I conducted a 3C analysis and identified the key consumer need: the desire to see the actual color swatches online. Based on this insight, I improved the PDP by adding a foundation color swatch feature, allowing consumers to directly compare shades.

Additionally, I volunteered with the CS team to provide direct support and color recommendations by responding to inquiries. I noticed a repetitive pattern in consumer inquiries. To resolve this, I utilized the data collected and developed a tree-structured chatbot prototype using Kakao i Builder, enabling automated consultations.
